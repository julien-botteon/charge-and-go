What is Charge&Go ?

Charge&GO is a young start-up created by students of Kedge Business School with the aim of developing the electrical market. Thus, they are trying to meet some of the present needs of unmet consumers and those of the future. These needs are: the lack of charging stations in some places, and the public deployment of charging stations pay-per-use in the future.

Download our application or on the website, and book when you want, where you want a charging point at a private home!
